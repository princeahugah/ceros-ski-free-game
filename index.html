  • Game Rules

    • Your Name Please enter your name when requested to do so. You don't want to miss the league table.

    • Game Status The game can have the status start, pause, playing, crashed. To pause, press the space bar

    • Game Score The score is calculated based on total the distance traveled.

    • Game Level There are 10 levels in this game. Each level requires that you cover a certain distance. When you move from one level to the other, the distance to be covered for the new level is recalculated since your speed also increases. You win the game when you finish the 10th level.

    • Game Speed The speed of the game increases when you move from one level to the other. The new speed is 50% more of the old speed.

    • Total Collisions The game allows you 5 collisions. The game ends after the 5th collision.

    • Game Controls Use the left, right, up and down as your controls. There are 3 buttons at your top right. The first is the leader board button. Next is the Game rules button. The last button is to reset the game.
